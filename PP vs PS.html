Grammar Guide

Past Simple

Used for finished actions at a specific time in the past.

  • Finished time (yesterday, in 2010)
  • Narrating stories
  • Finished habits

"I went to Paris in 2015."

Present Perfect

Connects past to present. Time is not specified.

  • Experiences (ever/never)
  • Recent actions (just/already)
  • Unfinished time (today, this week)

"I have been to Paris twice."
